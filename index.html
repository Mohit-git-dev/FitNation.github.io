Stress Relief

Yoga relieves stress and menstrual pain by calming the nervous system, improving blood circulation, and balancing hormones. Specific poses target the lower abdomen, reduce muscle cramps, and alleviate tension, promoting overall relaxation and comfort.

Period Cramps

Exercise helps reduce period cramps by increasing blood flow and releasing endorphins, which act as natural painkillers. Regular physical activity also relaxes muscles, reduces tension, and decreases prostaglandin levels, lowering cramp intensity.
